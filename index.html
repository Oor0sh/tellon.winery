Tellon Winery

There are no great enologists. Only good grapes. - Michel Rolland

Our wines are made from our highest quality grapes grown with a lot of care and love. Grapevines are easy to grow, but the vines need very good care to produce high quality fruit.

Wine is bottled poetry. - Robert Louis Stevenson

Bottling can be defined as the last phase of the wine making process, but the wine is now ready for the last part of its journey which, from the cask, passing through a bottle, will take it to be poured in glasses.

Life is too short to drink cheap wine. - Cliff Hakim

We have a lot of exceptional wines as we cherry pick the very best ones we discover, and the whole ethos of our business is about quality first.
